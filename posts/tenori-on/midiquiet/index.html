Suppress Midi SysEx

December 08, 2019 tenori-on

This function stops the output of MIDI SysEx messages everytime you press a button in normal mode.

When using the TNR as a midi controller, many devices are overwhelmed by the torrent of SysEx’s coming out of the TNR. I’ve seen several solutions involving midi hardware filters, etc.

This release simply adds a “turn off sysex” option.

In remote mode, of course, the SysEx output is not suppressed - this would defeat the purpose of the remote mode.

Compatibility Issues

There shouldn’t be any. The Midi Sysex setting is saveable, and is loadable, from standard settings files, including default.
